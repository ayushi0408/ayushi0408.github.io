POOJA EDUCATION CAMPUS Situated at well developed C.T.M. area in Ahmedabad city.
It runs different educational branches such as Pooja pre-primary school,Pooja Primary school,
Pooja secondary school, Pooja Higher Secondary school, Saffron English medium
school, Shree Umiya College of Education (B.Ed).All these branches are conducted by
Trust and Umiya Education Trust. We are providing the best education to the
students of our area.The students study here are belong to middle class families. We
provide them affordable education. We have the most experienced and competent Teachers
and Trainers who enable the students to face any kind of hardship. Our teachers always try
to encourage the students to participate in different educational and co-curriculer activities.
We celebrate different national and religious festivals with great enthusiasm with our students.Our
institute is working for the overall development of our students. Our purpose is
to prepare the best citizens and sculptors for the society.Our B.Ed. college has successfully
completed 8 years in preparing excellent teachers with proper responsibility and
tough efforts. We will do the same in future too.
